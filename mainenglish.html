Our History

Salvador, convinced that learning should not cost money, met a boy who could not study because he had to work to help out at home. This inspired him to create “Español Sin Fronteras”, a 100% free platform.

Here you can learn Spanish, finance, programming, math and science with more than 60 chapters full of content. Its mission is simple: that no one is left without studying for lack of time or money. If you want to grow and learn, “Spanish Without Borders” is for you.

Since its launch, “Español Sin Fronteras” has been used in more than 70 countries, demonstrating that the desire to learn has no borders.
